The official Computer Science House Drink app for iOS.

Need a refreshing soda or energy drink? Need a snack? No fear, just pull out your phone and drop what you want!

Key features:
• List items in all CSH drink and snack machines
• Drop any item immediately or after a delay
• View history of drops
• Monitor account credits

Note: You must be a member of Computer Science House to use the app.
